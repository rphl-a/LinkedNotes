## Related Definitions

| Definition                                                                                                                                               | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Agency of the Government.md\|Agency of the Government]]                                           | refers to any of the various units of the Government, including a department, bureau, office, instrumentality, or government-owned or controlled corporation, or a local government or a distinct unit therein.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Bureau.md\|Bureau]]                                                                               | refers to any principal subdivision or unit of any department. For purposes of Book IV, this shall include any principal subdivision or unit of any instrumentality given or assigned the rank of a bureau, regardless of actual name or designation, as in the case of department-wide regional offices.                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Chartered institution.md\|Chartered institution]]                                                 | refers to any agency organized or operating under a special charter, and vested by law with functions relating to specific constitutional policies or objectives. This term includes the state universities and colleges and the monetary authority of the State.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Department.md\|Department]]                                                                       | refers to an executive department created by law. For purposes of Book IV, this shall include any instrumentality, as herein defined, having or assigned the rank of a department, regardless of its name or designation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Employee.md\|Employee]]                                                                           | “Employee” when used with reference to a person in the public service, includes any person in the service of the government or any of its agencies, divisions, subdivisions or instrumentalities.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Government of the Republic of the Philippines.md\|Government of the Republic of the Philippines]] | refers to the corporate governmental entity through which the functions of government are exercised throughout the Philippines, including, save as the contrary appears from the context, the various arms through which political authority is made effective in the Philippines, whether pertaining to the autonomous regions, the provincial, city, municipal or barangay subdivisions or other forms of local government.                                                                         |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Instrumentality.md\|Instrumentality]]                                                             | refers to any agency of the National Government, not integrated within the department framework vested with special functions or jurisdiction by law, endowed with some if not all corporate powers, administering special funds, and enjoying operational autonomy, usually through a charter. This term includes regulatory agencies, chartered institutions and government-owned or controlled corporations.                                                                                       |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Local Agency.md\|Local Agency]]                                                                   | refers to a local government or a district unit therein                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Local Government.md\|Local Government]]                                                           | refers to the political subdivisions established by or in accordance with the Constitution.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/National Agency.md\|National Agency]]                                                             | refers to a unit of the National Government                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/National Government.md\|National Government]]                                                     | refers to the entire machinery of the central government, as distinguished from the different forms of local governments.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Office.md\|Office]]                                                                               | refers, within the framework of governmental organization, to any major functional unit of a department or bureau including regional offices. It may also refer to any position held or occupied by individual persons, whose functions are defined by law or regulation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Officer.md\|Officer]]                                                                             | “Officer” as distinguished from “clerk” or “employee”, refers to a person whose duties, not being of a clerical or manual nature, involves the exercise of discretion in the performance of the functions of the government. When used with reference to a person having authority to do a particular act or perform a particular function in the exercise of governmental power, “officer” includes any government employee, agent or body having authority to do the act or exercise that function. |
| [[11 - Philippine Statutes/Administrative Code of 1987/Regulatory agency.md\|Regulatory agency]]                                                         | refers to any agency expressly vested with jurisdiction to regulate, administer or adjudicate matters affecting substantial rights and interest of private persons, the principal powers of which are exercised by a collective body, such as a commission, board or council.                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
